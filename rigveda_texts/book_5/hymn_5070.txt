Title: HYMN LXX. Mitra-Varuṇa.
URL: https://sacred-texts.com/hin/rigveda/rv05070.htm
Book: 5, Hymn: 5070
==================================================

. EVEN far and wide, O Varuṇa and Mitra, doth your grace extend.May I obtain your kind good-will. From you, benignant Gods, may we gain fully food for sustenance.Such, O ye Rudras, my we be. Guard us, O Rudras. with your guar save us, ye skilled to save, my weSubdue the Dasyus, we ourselves, Or ne’er may we, O Wondrous Strong, enjoy another's solemn feast,Ourselves, our sons, or progeny.
