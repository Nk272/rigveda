Title: HYMN XLIX. Indra-Bṛhaspati.
URL: https://sacred-texts.com/hin/rigveda/rv04049.htm
Book: 4, Hymn: 4049
==================================================

. DEAR is this offering in your mouth, O Indra and Bṛhaspati:Famed is the laud, the gladdening draught. This lovely Soma is effused, O Indra and Bṛhaspati,For you, to drink it and rejoice. As Soma-drinkers to our house come, Indra and Bṛhaspati-and Indra-to drink Soma juice. Vouchsafe us riches hundredfold, O Indra, and Bṛhaspati,With store of horses, thousandfold. O Indra. and Bṛhaspati, we call you when the meath is shed,With songs, to drink the Soma juice. Drink, Indra and Bṛhaspati, the Soma in the giver's house:Delight yourselves abiding there.
