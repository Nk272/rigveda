. To you I come with this mine adoration, and with a hymn I crave the Strong Ones' favourA hymn that truly makes you joyful, Maruts. Suppress your anger and unyoke your horses. Maruts, to you this laud with prayer and worship, formed in the mind and heart, ye Gods, is offered.Come ye to us, rejoicing in your spirit, for ye are they who make our prayer effective. The Maruts, praised by us, shall show us favour; Maghavan, lauded, shall be most propitious.Maruts,, may all our days that are to follow be very pleasant, lovely and triumphant. I fled interrorfrom this mighty Indra, my body trembling in alarm, O Maruts.Oblations meant for you had been made ready; these have we set aside: for this forgive us.
